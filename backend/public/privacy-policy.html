Privacy Policy for Wedo App

Effective Date: May 1, 2026

Welcome to Wedo. Your privacy is of paramount importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application (the "App") and related services.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with a safe and efficient ride-sharing experience, we collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and profile picture (if provided).
  • Location Data: We collect precise and approximate location data to track rides, find nearby drivers, and ensure safety. This includes background location tracking during an active trip.
  • Financial Information: Payment details and transaction history for wallet top-ups and ride payments.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and App version for troubleshooting and performance monitoring.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your user account.
  • To match you with nearby drivers and calculate fares.
  • To process payments and manage your in-app wallet balance.
  • To ensure the safety of both riders and drivers through real-time tracking.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share your information in the following situations:

  • With Drivers: We share your name, pickup location, and destination with your assigned driver to facilitate the ride.
  • Service Providers: We may share data with secure third-party payment processors to handle transactions.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the safety and rights of our users or the public.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption in transit, to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights and Data Deletion

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. You can request the deletion of your account and associated data directly through the App settings or by contacting our support team. Upon a deletion request, we will remove your personal data, except where retention is required by law.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App, and the "Effective Date" at the top of this page will be updated. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
