Account Deletion Request

If you wish to delete your Wedo account and all associated personal data, please follow the steps below.

How to Delete Your Account

You can request account deletion using one of the following methods:

Method 1: Inside the App (Recommended)

  1. Open the Wedo App and log in to your account.
  2. Go to the Menu or Profile section.
  3. Tap on Settings.
  4. Scroll down and tap on Delete Account.
  5. Confirm your decision. Your account and data will be permanently deleted.

Method 2: Contact Support

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request account deletion by emailing our support team:

  • Email: support@wedo-app.com
  • Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Body: Please include your full name and the phone number/email associated with your Wedo account.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

Upon verifying your request, we will delete the following data associated with your account:

  • Personal information (Name, Phone number, Email address, Profile picture)
  • Saved locations and addresses
  • Payment methods and wallet balance (Note: Ensure you have withdrawn or used any balance before requesting deletion, as it cannot be recovered).

Data Kept for Legal/Security Reasons

Please note that we may retain certain information (such as transaction history and trip logs) for a legally required period (e.g., 7 years for financial and taxation purposes) or to prevent fraud. This data will be anonymized where possible and stored securely.
