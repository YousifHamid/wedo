# Wedo Pitch Deck + Investment & Valuation

## 1. Overview
Wedo is a ride-hailing platform tailored for Sudan, designed to handle weak infrastructure while delivering a modern user experience.

## 2. Problem
- Unorganized transport market
- Unstable pricing
- Weak current apps
- Low driver income

## 3. Solution
- Real-time connection between riders & drivers
- Works under weak internet (Upfront Fixed Pricing)
- Balanced pricing model

## 4. Product
- Unified app (Rider + Driver seamless switching)
- Live tracking + Safety & SOS features
- Flexible Wallet + Cash system

## 5. Technology
- **Frontend:** React Native (Expo) - Single Repo
- **Backend:** Node.js + Express + MongoDB
- **Real-Time Engine:** Socket.io + Redis Cache

## 6. Market Opportunity
- Large underserved Sudanese market
- Expansion to MENA & Africa

## 7. Business Model
- Commission per ride (Lower than competitors to attract drivers)
- Wallet services & direct digital prepayments
- Future ads/subscriptions

## 8. Competitive Advantage
- 8–12% cheaper for passengers than competitors (Tirhal, Yango)
- Higher net earnings for drivers
- Hyper-localized experience

## 9. Legal Value
- Full source code ownership (Proprietary asset)
- Ready for immediate company registration
- Trademark & brand protection possible
- Contracts & policies ready

## 10. Marketing Value
- Strong viral potential through integrated growth loops
- Clear problem-solution fit
- Low-cost growth potential

## 11. Current Real Value
- MVP fully functional and production-ready
- Market-ready concept successfully mapped
- Reduced technical risk for early investors

## 12. Estimated Valuation
Based on the advanced functional MVP stage explicitly built for emerging markets:
- **Estimated value:** $100,000 – $300,000

## 13. Investment Options (Sudan-compatible)
- Equity Investment (10%–25%)
- Partnership Model
- Convertible Agreement (future equity)

## 14. Investment Ask
- **Suggested raise:** $25,000 – $75,000
**Use of funds:**
- Marketing & driver acquisition
- Cloud infrastructure
- Ground Operations

## 15. Vision
To become the leading ride-hailing platform in Sudan and expand across Africa.
