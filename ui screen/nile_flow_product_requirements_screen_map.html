# Project: Nile Flow - Ride-Hailing System (Sudan) ## 1. Overview A specialized ride-hailing system for Sudan, optimized for low bandwidth, cash payments, and zone-based pricing. The system consists of three distinct parts: a Rider App, a Driver App, and an Admin Dashboard. --- ## 2. Rider App Flows (Mobile) **Goal:** Book a reliable ride using cash, with minimal data usage. * **Screen 1: Splash & OTP Login** * Simple phone number input. * Fast OTP verification (standard for Sudan). * **Screen 2: Home / Booking (Zone-Based)** * Pickup and Drop-off selection using a list of predefined zones (not map-dependent). * Estimated fare (fixed zone-to-zone price). * "Request Ride" button (Cash payment only). * **Screen 3: Searching & Driver Assigned** * Animation for searching. * Once assigned: Driver name, car model, plate number, and ETA. * Call driver button. * **Screen 4: Trip in Progress & Completion** * Simple status: "En route to destination." * Completion screen with final fare (to be paid in cash) and rating. --- ## 3. Driver App Flows (Mobile) **Goal:** Accept trips and manage a prepaid wallet. * **Screen 1: Dashboard & Go Online** * Toggle for Online/Offline status. * Current wallet balance (must be > 0 to receive trips). * Quick link to Top-up wallet. * **Screen 2: Incoming Trip Request** * 15-second timer to accept. * Pickup and Drop-off zones. * Expected fare and commission deduction. * **Screen 3: Active Trip Management** * "Arrived at Pickup" button. * "Start Trip" and "Complete Trip" buttons. * Navigation link (Google Maps/Waze). * **Screen 4: Wallet & Earnings** * Detailed transaction history (Trips, Commissions, Top-ups). * Instructions for manual cash top-ups at designated centers. --- ## 4. Admin Dashboard (Desktop) **Goal:** Manage zones, drivers, and wallet top-ups. * **Screen 1: Overview & Stats** * Active trips, online drivers, total revenue (commission). * Real-time system health monitor. * **Screen 2: Driver Management** * List of all drivers with status (Active, Blocked, Pending Approval). * Approval workflow for new drivers. * **Screen 3: Wallet Top-up Approvals** * Queue of driver top-up requests (Cash deposits). * Approve/Reject buttons to update driver balances. * **Screen 4: Zone & Pricing Management** * Define zones and set fixed prices between them. * Manage commission rates. --- ## 5. Technical Considerations * **Matching Engine:** Sequential dispatch (requests the nearest driver first, then the next). * **Wallet Logic:** Commissions are automatically deducted after every trip. If the balance reaches 0, the driver is automatically toggled offline. * **Low Bandwidth:** Minimal images, optimized API calls, and heavy caching of zone data.
